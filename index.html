01.About Me

I believe the hardest part of building AI isn't the technology — it's keeping people at the center of it. That's the thread that runs through my work.

I lead with empathy — for the users whose workflows I simplify and the teams I build with. But empathy doesn't mean going easy: when I'm handed a problem, I get attentive and demanding, digging into details and holding the work to a high bar. I'm a stickler for the small things, and I read a lot of psychology — understanding how people think makes me better at building things they'll actually use.

What keeps me effective in a field that reinvents itself every few months is a willingness to learn — and unlearn. And off the clock I'm happiest in motion: a state-level badminton player, trained classical dancer, painter, and a new runner who feels alive after a 2K.
